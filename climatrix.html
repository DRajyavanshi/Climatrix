ℹ️ About Climatrix

AI-Powered Climate Change Impact Prediction Platform — Galgotias University

🌍 About the Project

Climatrix is an AI-based Climate Change Impact Prediction System developed at Galgotias University for the Environmental Impact Analysis course (C1UB120T). It provides accessible, hyper-regional climate intelligence for India — moving beyond static averages to deliver machine learning-driven forecasts.

The platform bridges the gap between complex climate science and real-world decision-making by providing actionable, easy-to-understand insights for communities, farmers, planners, and policymakers.
