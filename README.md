Climatrix
A Climate Change Impact Prediction System with AI Impact Assessment. 
Problem

Climate change is causing major environmental instability across regions. Rising temperatures, irregular rainfall, and increasing extreme weather events are impacting agriculture, water resources, and human life. Although climate data platforms exist, many are too complex, enterprise-focused, and lack regional context, making them difficult for common users to access and understand.

Solution

Climatrix is an AI-powered climate impact prediction system that analyzes historical climate data and forecasts potential environmental risks. it predicts climate trends, estimate extreme weather probabilities, and classify regions based on risk levels, presenting the results through an interactive and user-friendly dashboard.

Features

Climate Prediction – Forecast future temperature, rainfall, and humidity trends
Extreme Weather Estimation – Predict probability of events like heatwaves or storms
Regional Risk Classification – Categorize areas into low, medium, or high climate risk
Interactive Dashboard – Visualize climate data and trends for better understanding
Early Warning Insights – Help users plan for climate-related risks and disasters
